2. Suggested Template for Each Book (Two-Page Spread)

You don’t need all sections every time, but having a consistent pattern will make the book smooth to read.

A. Context

Short author sketch (background, reputation, where this book sits in their life’s work)

Year written and historical moment

When the journey took place

B. The Journey

The route

Key scenes

What the writer was seeking

C. Themes and Insights

What makes this book more than a travelogue

What the writer discovers about place, self, or culture

D. Why It’s Essential

Its influence on travel writing

Why it endures

Who will love it

E. Reading Notes

Style of writing

Pacing and tone

Any useful edition or translator notes

F. Pairings / Further Reading
One or two books that pair well. Example: “If you like The Snow Leopard, try…”.

G. Memorable Line
A single quote (fair-use length) that captures the voice.
